Welcome Aboard!

Last year, 1500 youth across the world were touched with the spirit of service and spirituality during the 2 day Youth Festival held at the Shrimad Rajchandra Mission Dharampur Headquarters, the Ashram. Here, youth explored the values of Satsang, Seva, Sanskruti, Sadhana, and Sports. Pujya Gurudevshri’s flow of love is such that even a global pandemic cannot stop it.

While we cannot meet in person, the spread of love, care, and spirituality will take a new form, this time, online. On the day of love, February 14th, the youth across the world will once again experience the the joy of service, the light of wisdom, and the power of divine love through the 24-hour Virtual Youth Festival: One Youth, One Love.
