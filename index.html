WHAT ARE YOU IN FOR?

Celebrating the day of love, a 24-hour curated confluence of 14 world-renowned mentors and motivators will inspire global youth towards inner growth and dynamic action.

WHY ARE WE COMING TOGETHER?

To reflect on the new normal and evoke a greater purpose as a collective.

To draw on our global unity across mindsets, cultures and industries, and resolve for a positive, peaceful and better future.

WHERE WILL WE CONNECT?

Youth from 14 countries will walk into a virtual auditorium for a cutting-edge, digital experience. A simulated festival layout with convention halls, stalls and exhibit areas.

KEYNOTE ADDRESS

Pujya Gurudevshri Rakeshbhai

Enlightened Master & Founder, SRMD

A spiritual visionary, a modern-day mystic, and an ardent devotee of Shrimad Rajchandraji, He is the founder of Shrimad Rajchandra Mission Dharampur – a spiritual movement with 108 centres across five continents.

Blending theory with experience and the head with the heart, Pujya Gurudevshri provides powerful and practical tools to joyfully tread the inward journey. He is empowering and transforming millions across the globe with the mission statement – Realise one’s True Self and serve others selflessly

Fondly known as a leader of the youth, Pujya Gurudevshri harnesses the creative potential and intelligence of young hearts, steering them towards a higher goal through the 5S programme.

“Tremendous energy, enthusiasm, exuberance, and power are the assets of today’s youth. They have the drive but need the direction. They have the wings and if given the eyes they can soar high and far in the spiritual skies.”
-Pujya Gurudevshri

Pujya Gaur Gopal Das

Monk & Life Coach, ISKCON

A practising monk for the past 23 years, he is a storyteller, life coach and author with videos that have collectively racked up over 500 million views. His charming personality coupled with a simplicity in imparting spirituality has won hearts worldwide, including the youth. He has also partnered with institutions like Google, Salesforce, Bombay Stock Exchange, to name a few.

Pujya Gyanvatsal Swami

Saint & Social Reformer, BAPS

A part of BAPS (Bochasanwasi Akshar Purushottam Swaminarayan Sanstha), he is a motivational speaker, saint, and social reformer. A modern-day monk, he has delivered life-changing speeches around the globe on relevant topics like stress management, work-life balance, ethics in profession, to name a few.

Brahma Kumari Sister Shivani

Senior Rajyoga Teacher & International Motivational Speaker

A renowned spiritual mentor, she is the recipient of the prestigious Nari Shakti Puraskaar, the highest civilian honour for women in India, for her role in transforming human behaviours. She has made spirituality easy, understandable and relatable through her acclaimed television series, ‘Awakening with the Brahmakumaris’.

Dr. Deepak Chopra TM

MD, FACP & Best-selling Author

He is the founder of the Chopra Foundation, a non-profit entity for research on well-being and humanitarianism, and Chopra Global, a whole health company at the intersection of science and spirituality. He is a pioneer of integrative medicine and author of over ninety books published in more than forty-three languages. Many have been New York Times bestsellers in both the fiction and nonfiction categories.

Idalene Kesner, Ph.D

How is the festival designed?

Spiritual Wisdom

Gain profound knowledge and practical insights directly from the most experienced, influential, and loved spiritual masters and mentors of the world.

Inspiring Sessions

Widen your horizons through engaging talks by featured speakers including high-performance achievers, opinion makers, and industry leaders.

The Cause

Women empowerment is an initiative of ​Shrimad Rajchandra Love and Care.​

SRLC is the charitable ten-fold programme of Shrimad Rajchandra Mission Dharampur​. It has been granted ‘Special Consultative Status’ by the United Nations Economic and Social Council (UN ECOSOC) and promotes UN's Sustainable Development Goals like no poverty, gender equality, decent work and economic growth. SRLC supports underprivileged tribal women in their quest towards attaining self-reliance and a respectable source of income.

They are offered end-to-end support through employment opportunities, skill training, raw material and equipment, safe workplaces, as well as product marketing and distribution. These dedicated women produce over 100 desserts, snacks, fragrances and utility products.

All proceeds from the Global Youth Festival 2021 will directly support in uplifting the lives of more and more tribal women in need.
