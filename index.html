WHAT

Celebrating the day of love, a 24-hour curated confluence of 14 world-renowned mentors and motivators will inspire global youth towards inner growth and dynamic action

WHY

To reflect on the new normal and evoke a greater purpose as a collective.

To draw on our global unity across mindsets, cultures and industries, and resolve for a positive, peaceful and better future.

WHERE

Youth from 14 countries will walk into a virtual auditorium for a cutting-edge, digital experience. A simulated festival layout with convention halls, stalls and exhibit areas.

KEYNOTE SPEAKER

Pujya Gurudevshri Rakeshbhai

A spiritual visionary, a modern-day mystic, and an ardent devotee of Shrimad Rajchandraji, He is the founder of Shrimad Rajchandra Mission Dharampur – a spiritual movement with 108 centres across five continents.

Blending theory with experience and the head with the heart, Pujya Gurudevshri provides powerful and practical tools to joyfully tread the inward journey. He is empowering and transforming millions across the globe with the mission statement – Realise one’s True Self and serve others selflessly

Fondly known as a leader of the youth, Pujya Gurudevshri harnesses the creative potential and intelligence of young hearts, steering them towards a higher goal through the 5S programme.

“Tremendous energy, enthusiasm, exuberance, and power are the assets of today’s youth. They have the drive but need the direction. They have the wings and if given the eyes they can soar high and far in the spiritual skies.”
-Pujya Gurudevshri

Gaur Gopal Das

Monk & Life Coach, ISKCON

A practicing monk for the past 23 years, he is a storyteller, life coach and author with videos that have collectively racked up over 500 million views. His charming personality coupled with a simplicity in imparting spirituality has won hearts worldwide, including the youth. He has also partnered with institutions like Google, Salesforce, Bombay Stock Exchange, to name a few.

Gyanvatsal Swami

Saint & Social Reformer, BAPS

Pujya Shri Gyanvatsal Swami, who is a part of the BAPS (Bochasanwasi Akshar Purushottam Swaminarayan Sanstha), is a motivational speaker, spiritual leader and a social reformer. A modern-day monk, he has delivered life-changing speeches around the globe on relevant topics like stress management, work-life balance, ethics in profession, to name a few.

Sister Shivani

Senior Rajyoga Teacher & International Motivational Speaker

Brahma Kumari Sister Shivani is a globally renowned spiritual guide and mentor. She is the recipient of the prestigious Nari Shakti Puraskaar, the highest civilian honour for women in India, for her role in transforming human behaviours and has been appointed as a Goodwill Ambassador by the World Psychiatric Association. Sister Shivani normalised the conversation around matters like ego, stress, anger and more through the riveting television series, ‘Awakening with the Brahmakumaris’, making spirituality easy, understandable, and relatable for millions across the globe.

Deepak Chopra

MD, FACP & Best-selling Author

He is the founder of the Chopra Foundation, a non-profit entity for research on well-being and humanitarianism, and Chopra Global, a whole health company at the intersection of science and spirituality. He is a pioneer of integrative medicine and author of over ninety books published in more than forty-three languages. Many have been New York Times bestsellers in both the fiction and nonfiction categories.

Idalene Kesner

Dean, Kelley School of Business

Idalene Kesner is the Dean at The Kelley School of Business. A visionary, trail-blazer, mentor, and a groundbreaking educator, Dean Kesner has successfully paved the way for a more diverse, inclusive and pro-female educational institution. She was awarded the Dean of the Year in 2019 by Poets & Quants, a leading news site about business education.

Kuntal Joisher

World’s First Vegan To Scale Mt. Everest

Kuntal Joisher, an avid climber, risk-taker, and motivational speaker, is famously known as the first vegan to climb Mount Everest. But, he is first and foremost, a dreamer. He has reached great heights, both literally and figuratively, and today, his journey inspires scores across the globe to chase their dreams, pursue their passions and live compassionately!

Dr. Aditi Govitrikar

Actor, Model & Psychologist

The only Indian supermodel-actor with a medical and psychologist’s qualification; Dr. Aditi Govitrikar in a true sense is ‘Beauty with Brains’ – a title given to her by Business Today, and other leading publications. Having won accolades like Gladrags Supermodel & Mrs. World, Dr. Aditi has also acted in Bollywood and Telugu films. Dr. Aditi currently practices as a certified counsellor and mentors corporates with transformational workshops.

Vikram Hazra

Devotional Musician

A devotee of Sri Sri Ravi Shankar, Vikram Hazra is the Programme Director for the International Art of Living Foundation. His music is a beautiful amalgamation of spiritual songs with a modern-day edge. Inviting listeners to rise in love for the divine, his contemporary take on devotional music has enamoured listeners globally at concerts in over 40 countries!

Aarti Shah, Ph.D

Senior VP & Chief Information and Digital Officer, Eli Lilly and Company

She is a key member in the executive committee of Eli Lilly & Company, a global healthcare leader and a Fortune 500 company. She is an accomplished executive with 25+ years of extensive experience in drug development and commercialization, technology, cyber, digital transformation and data sciences. She is also known for her inspirational and genuine leadership style, and for leading global cross-functional teams of several thousand employees from vision development to strategy creation to execution.

Dr. Mansi Shah

SRMD Yoga

Dr. Mansi Shah, MD FAAP is a spiritual seeker originally from Arizona, USA. She is a practicing pediatrician and is also a registered yoga teacher with Yoga Alliance. She completed her Yoga Teacher’s Training Program in classical ashtanga and hatha at World Peace Yoga School in Rishikesh. Within yoga, her passion lies in yoga with children and yoga anatomy. Mansi takes yoga sessions at the Dharampur Ashram and leads workshops in Mumbai.

Atmarpit Shraddhaben

SRMD Yoga

Atmarpit Shraddhaben is the youngest swadhyaykar of Shrimad Rajchandra Mission Dharampur. She has completed her BA in Philosophy with special focus on Jainology and is an accredited Yoga trainer and Yoga teacher’s trainer in Shastriya Ashtang yoga. Born in Antwerp, Belgium, she shifted to India for spiritual growth and in 2017, embraced Atmarpit Diksha. Her sessions are focused on experiential and applicable teachings of the Enlightened ones.

The Cause

Raj Uphaar​ is an initiative of ​Shrimad Rajchandra Love and Care​, the charitable ten-fold programme of ​Shrimad Rajchandra Mission Dharampur​

This initiative supports over 100 underprivileged tribal women in their quest towards attaining self-reliance and a respectable source of income.

They are offered end-to-end support through employment opportunities, skill training, raw material and equipment, safe workplaces, as well as product marketing and distribution. These dedicated women produce more than 100 desserts, snacks, fragrances and utility products.

All proceeds from the Global Youth Festival 2021 will directly support Raj Uphaar in empowering and uplifting the lives of more and more tribal women in need.
